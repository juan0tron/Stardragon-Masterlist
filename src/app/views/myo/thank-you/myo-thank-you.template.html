Make sure to submit your MYO to DevArt Stash, Imgur, Dropbox, or some other private medium. You may post it publicly once it’s been accepted and submitted to the FurAffinity MYO group.

You may used the attached placeholder image to keep track of unused slots on sites like Deviantart and Toyhouse.

You may trade or sell your MYO slot.

You may only sell a MYO for it's original sale price.

When you submit a design and it is approved, your SLOT becomes a DESIGN. You may not destroy or completely redesign a DESIGN or treat it as you would a SLOT just because it was a MYO.

Minor edits to MYO DESIGNs are allowed within reason.

You may add unlimited traits within your rarity tier.

You may add traits from your rarity tier, as well as all tiers considered below it. (i.e. a Rare slot have rare, uncommon, and common traits.)

If you get a uncommon spot, you CANNOT put a rare or legendary on it. You can only go down tiers, not up tiers.
